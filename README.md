
# 📊 Sales Analysis Project – Retail Dataset

## 📌 Project Overview
This project performs an end-to-end **Sales Analysis** using a retail (Superstore) dataset to uncover business insights.  
The analysis focuses on **revenue performance, top products, seasonality trends, regional sales, and key KPIs**, supported by data visualizations and a one-page summary report.

The project is designed to simulate a **real-world business analytics task** and demonstrates practical skills in **data cleaning, analysis, visualization, and insight generation**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze retail sales data to answer key business questions
- Identify **top-performing products**
- Analyze **seasonality and sales trends**
- Compute important **business KPIs**
- Compare **regional performance**
- Provide **data-driven business recommendations**
- Export a **one-page PDF summary** with insights

---

## 🧰 Tools & Technologies Used
- **Python**
- **Pandas** – data manipulation
- **NumPy** – numerical operations
- **Matplotlib & Seaborn** – data visualization
- **FPDF** – PDF report generation

---

## 📂 Dataset Information
- **Dataset Name:** Sample Superstore Dataset
- **Type:** Retail sales data
- **Format:** CSV
- **Key Columns:**
  - Order Date
  - Order ID
  - Product Name
  - Category
  - Region
  - Sales
  - Quantity
  - Profit

---

## 📊 Key KPIs Calculated
- **Total Revenue**
- **Average Order Value (AOV)**
- **Top Products by Sales**
- **Top Regions by Revenue**
- **Monthly Sales Trends**

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed
1. Data loading and inspection  
2. Data cleaning and date formatting  
3. Feature engineering (month, year extraction)  
4. KPI computation  
5. Product-wise sales analysis  
6. Monthly seasonality analysis  
7. Regional sales comparison  
8. Time-series sales trend analysis  

---

## 📈 Visualizations
- Bar chart of **Top 10 Products**
- Line chart showing **Monthly Sales Trends**
- Bar chart of **Regional Sales Performance**
- Time-series line chart of **Sales Over Time**

---

## 🧠 Key Insights
- Sales show **strong seasonality**, peaking in year-end months
- A small number of products contribute to a large share of revenue
- Certain regions significantly outperform others
- Average Order Value indicates opportunities for upselling and bundling

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Increase marketing efforts during high-sales months
- Focus promotional strategies on top-performing products
- Expand operations and inventory in high-revenue regions
- Introduce product bundles to improve Average Order Value

---

## 📄 Project Output
- Multiple data visualizations
- KPI metrics summary
- **One-page PDF summary report** containing:
  - Revenue figures
  - Insights
  - Business recommendations
